Become Someone of Worth!

Plant Trees

Plant a tree—or just about anything. Everyone talks about reducing carbon emissions, but nature is the best carbon sink there is. So instead of just reducing: reabsorb. Reducing slash-and-burn agriculture would help too.

Donate

You can do really no better than to donate to our world-altering cause. Every penny will go towards furthering our interests. Remember the more you donate the more Worthy you become—this is definitely not an area where you can afford to be be cheap.

Become a Vegetarian

Become a vegetarian. Eating higher-up on the food-chain is costly: to the environment, to your health, to your wallet. Think of all the extra money you'll be able to donate to us—and feel good while doing it.

No Fires

Avoid fires of any kind,—bonfires, BBQs, fireworks—particularly during the height of the dry-season in densely wooded areas. This issue is two-fold: not only is the risk to forests—a very significant carbon sink—of deforestation high, but fires (and especially forest fires) emit green-house gases. (Note: This warning goes double for any 2nd Amendment advocates whose pyrotechnics displays employ TND's*.)

*ThermoNuclear Devices

No Mobile (P)Ho(m/n)es

No mobile homes: These are terrible. Do you have any idea how environmentally unfriendly (EU) it is to drag a house from one side of a continent to another? You're not a snail! If you must "commune with nature" use a tent. And, while you're at it, get rid of your mobile phone: they are also EU and cost a lot of money, which you could otherwise give to us.
