Why We Exist

It is our purpose to...

bring attention to climate change in the hope that more sustainable environmental practices can be brought about.

Our goal is to educate, and then let an educated public make up its own minds.

We are a non-profit organization. That, however, does not mean that we will not accept donations, handouts, endorsed pension cheques, or other forms of negotiable security... bribes, or just about any form of lucre we might get our hands on. We'll even take your hawkable items (ideally those that would be revered by the Christie's/Sotheby's set). We're about the environment—not ethics. All's fair and legal when it's for a good cause.

So, give us your money, and then get out there and make a difference... or not.
